This github repository contains resources for secondary analysis of data from cross-national survey projects, including ex-post survey data harmonization. In particular, I collected survey metadata in form of data dictionaries or codebooks, from two major cross-national survey projects: the Eurobarometer and the International Social Survey Programme, which are available here.
